Compared with before the median changed but the mean stayed the same. This is due to the effect that imputation does not affect the mean since it replaces all NAs with the mean value. The median changed because the number of values increased by replacing NAs with the mean value so the exact number in the middle became different (50% value of number order). The histogram now shows more values as days with NAs were filled with mean value.
